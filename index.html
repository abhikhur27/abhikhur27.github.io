Article scaffolds and research tracks

These are portfolio-ready outlines prepared for long-form technical writing. Each includes an angle, structure, and source plan.

Public Transportation Systems: Why Transfer Penalties Matter More Than Top Speed

Outline: rider decision model, network friction, case study comparison, policy implications.

Key Sources: Transit Capacity and Quality of Service Manual, GTFS feeds, city ridership dashboards.

Technical Angle: shortest path with weighted transfer costs, schedule reliability variance.

Rail Infrastructure: Dispatching Tradeoffs in Shared Passenger-Freight Corridors

Outline: signal blocks, headways, conflict windows, simulation-backed interventions.

Key Sources: FRA safety reports, Amtrak OTP data, UIC infrastructure guidelines.

Technical Angle: discrete-event rail dispatch simulation and throughput sensitivity analysis.

Paleontology: What Dinosaur Bonebeds Reveal About Population Dynamics

Outline: preservation bias, age distribution signals, environmental context, interpretation limits.

Key Sources: Society of Vertebrate Paleontology papers, museum open datasets.

Technical Angle: Bayesian inference for fossil sampling bias and uncertainty bounds.

Etymology: How Sound Shifts Encode Migration and Contact

Outline: phoneme drift patterns, borrowing signatures, timeline reconstruction.

Key Sources: Oxford English Dictionary, etymological dictionaries, historical corpora.

Technical Angle: graph-based lineage tracing and edit-distance clustering of cognates.

Sports Analytics: Shot Quality vs Shot Making in Modern Basketball

Outline: expected value models, role context, defensive pressure, lineup interactions.

Key Sources: NBA stats API, cleaning-the-glass style metrics, tracking datasets.

Technical Angle: logistic shot models, uncertainty intervals, possession-level aggregation.

Trading Systems: Volatility Regimes and Position Sizing Failures

Outline: regime shifts, drawdown anatomy, sizing policies, risk controls.

Key Sources: CBOE VIX series, FRED macro data, SEC market structure references.

Technical Angle: regime classifier, rolling correlation decomposition, stress tests.
